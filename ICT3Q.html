TLE-ICT LESSONS

4TH QTR.!

Lesson 1

I learned that typography is the art and technique of arranging type to make written language clear, readable, and visually appealing. It involves choosing appropriate fonts, sizes, spacing, and layout to effectively communicate a message and create a specific mood or tone.

Lesson 2

I learned that indenting text helps organize paragraphs neatly, while adding borders and background colors makes important information stand out visually. Creating lists helps present information clearly and makes content easier to read and understand.

Lesson 3

I learned that CSS layout uses the box model (content, padding, border, and margin) to control the size and spacing of elements, while properties like position, offset, and float help place elements precisely on a webpage. I also learned that background properties, margins, and attachment settings allow designers to control images, spacing, and how content moves or scrolls on the page.
